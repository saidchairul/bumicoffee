About us

It is an honor for us to introduce bumicoffee as a company engaged in domestic and international trade, especially Indonesian coffee commodity. Become one of the best coffee bean producing countries in the world makes us interested in selling coffee beans to domestic and international markets. we also help and educate farmers to maintain the quality of coffee beans so that the results remain optimal.

Our company started its business by being located in Makassar City, South Sulawesi, Indonesian.
